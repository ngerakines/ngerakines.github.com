About

Nick Gerakines (born Baton Rouge 1983) is an American author, speaker and software engineer. He is a contributor to a number of notable open source Erlang projects on GitHub and is the author of the book Facebook Application Development. -- Wikipedia

I'm pretty active on the usual social places including GitHub, del.icio.us, twitter and Facebook. I prefer email over instant messaging but will also respond to Facebook messages.

I fall into the "casual gamer" category and can be found playing the World of Warcraft character Korale. I've been featured on one of WoW Insider's 15 Minutes of Fame as well as a few others because of this Facebook application that I wrote called I Play WoW.
